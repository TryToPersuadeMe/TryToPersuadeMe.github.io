About us

One of the most famous Moscow boutique-ateliers “Mastersuit”, located on the Prechistenka street, has opened its cafe with a menu of European cuisine.

Once you open massive wooden doors, you will find yourself in a bright space with large windows overlooking one of the central streets of Moscow.

Alexander Keller worked on the bar list. He is also known for such projects like Mendeleev Bar, LADO, Strelka

Therefore, you can boldly go to Mastersuit Café for an aperitif.
