ETIAS

The European Travel Information and Authorisation System

It late 2022 for the Americans it will be mandatory to obtain ETIAS travel authorization to enter to the EU.

Once the new rules are implemented, the travelers will be able to enter to the Schengen zone only after filling out an online application and getting an ETIAS visa waiver certificate that gives a permission to stay and freely travel in the EU zone for up to 90 days.

The new regulations may take a lot of travelers by surprise. People who are used to visa-free entry into the territory of the EU may not be aware of the new requirement.

The initiative requires that all arriving passengers have valid ETIAS registration before arrival into the territory of the EU for short stays. ETIAS cannot be obtained last-minute or upon arrival, putting all unaware passengers at risk of missing their trips.

ETIAS applications require accurate and specific personal information, including medical condition, criminal records, employment history, past travel information etc.

Failure to provide accurate data will result in rejection of ETIAS or denial of entry into the EU. For this reason it is essential to ensure that all of the information correct before submitting the ETIAS application, as even small errors may result in the revocation of an ETIAS.

What is ETIAS visa waiver?

The European Travel Information and Authorisation System (ETIAS) is a visa waiver program that is supposedly going to be implemented in 2022. Its aim is to guarantee a safer traveling to the EU by monitoring the data of tourists before their enter to the EU zone.

It is important to note that ETIAS is not a visa. Also it will be proceeded online, so it doesn’t oblige travelers to show up in an embassy or any other physical organization.

Who needs an ETIAS visa waiver?

For now the citizens of 60 countries are supposed to get ETIAS and there is a high possibility that it is going to involve more countries. The countries which are visa-free now are not going to loose this status, however for the citizens it will be mandatory to have an ETIAS.

The citizens of not visa-free countries apart from getting an ETIAS will still need to apply for the Schengen visa or any other visa type that permits to stay in the Schengen zone (visa for students, workers etc.). Remember that ETIAS is not a visa replacement.

How does the ETIAS system work?

As said, the whole process of getting ETIAS will be done online.

Travelers will need to answers some questions. There are going to be basic questions (name, date of birth, passport data, address) and also several questions about security and health. All information will be 100% private.

Every applicants will get an ETIAS waiver electronically. Travelers will need to present it on the border with the Schengen zone.

Why a European travel authorisation?

The aim of ETIAS visa waiver is to guarantee the safety of the EU countries. Such a serious control will simplify the identification process of the potential risks, so the EU zone will be highly prevented from any kind of danger.
