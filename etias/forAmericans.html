ETIAS for Americans

The European authorities have already announced that the new European Travel Information and Authorisation System (ETIAS) will be live and into full effect by the end of 2022. To enter to the EU the citizens from over 60 countries will need to register online with ETIAS.

Even when ETIAS comes out in late 2022, it will not mean that Americans will need a visa for Europe, but for the Americans it will be mandatory to obtain ETIAS travel authorization to enter to the EU.

Remember that ETIAS is not a visa. is an electronical travel permit.

For the moment, the ETIAS requirements for the Americans are the followings:

  • proving a valid US passport
  • paying ETIAS-application fee
  • proving a valid e-mail address
  • illing out ETIAS-application form

For the moment, the ETIAS requirements for the Americans are the followings:

  • personal information (full name, place of birth, gender etc.)
  • contact details (address, phone number, email etc.)
  • passport details
  • the first country of entry to the Schengen Area and travel plans
  • additional necessary information (health issues, criminal record etc.)

ETIAS is applied to help the European authorities to strengthen border control and also to help the travelers to have fast and automated control on the border.

Note that ETIAS visa waiver does not guarantee entry to the Schengen zone. The final decision about the traveler’s eligibility to cross the border is up to the control officers.
